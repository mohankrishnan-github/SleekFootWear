About Us

Our Mission

Our vision is to build a destination that has vast selection, low price range, and above all, products that are nothing but world class and of premium quality.

World renowned footwear brands such as Adidas, Nike, Puma, Lee Cooper are combined in-store. So if you are looking for trainers, high heels, summer sandals, knee-high boots, ankle boots, casual shoes, work shoes, waterproof boots, etc - we’ll have something for you to choose from.

We also stock a great selection of luggage’s & handbags, from clutch bags for weddings, everyday bags & slouch bags and also school & college bags for kids & adult all from liopular brands.

Along with the brand names we also stock our own range of footwear’s, bags, purses & valets that offers customers a convenient way to shop for a wide selection of lifestyle products at attractive prices.
